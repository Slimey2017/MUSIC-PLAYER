Explicit Content & Age Tiers

FREQ uses a tiered content visibility system based on age and parental guidance status. All users see clean content by default. Access to mature or explicit material requires meeting the applicable age tier or having verified parental consent.

Tier Who Can Access Content Allowed
Clean All users (13+) No profanity, no mature themes. Default for all accounts.
Parental Guidance 13–17 with parental consent (coming soon) Mild language, moderate themes (Parents can pick which settings to enable like clean, recommended, or 18+). Parent/guardian must approve via Parental Controls.
Explicit (18+) Users 18 or older only Strong language, adult lyrical themes. Enabled manually in account settings.

Artist flagging responsibilities:

  • Artists must accurately flag their content as Clean, Parental Guidance, or Explicit (18+) at the time of upload or release creation.
  • Failure to flag content correctly may result in content removal, restricted visibility, or account penalties.
  • FREQ may re-classify content at its discretion if the artist-assigned rating is found to be inaccurate.

Explicit (18+) content rules:

  • Users must be 18 or older to enable explicit content visibility in account settings. By enabling this setting, you confirm under self-declaration that you meet this requirement.
  • The explicit flag applies to lyrical and thematic content only — strong language, drug references, violence references, and mature subject matter in lyrics.
  • FREQ does not allow sexually explicit audio or video under any flag setting. Graphic sexual content is prohibited regardless of artist rating.

Parental Guidance (13–17) — Coming Soon:

  • FREQ is developing a Parental Controls system that will allow parents or guardians to manage what content their child (ages 13–17) can access on the Platform.
  • Once live, parents will be able to restrict access to specific artists, songs, albums, and content tiers from a dedicated parental dashboard.
  • Until Parental Controls are available, accounts belonging to users aged 13–17 are restricted to Clean content by default. Parental Guidance content will only become accessible after the controls feature launches and a parent has explicitly approved it.
  • Parents or guardians who become aware their child is misrepresenting their age on FREQ should contact support to request account review.
⚠ Zero Tolerance — Minors

Content that sexualizes, exploits, or endangers minors in any form is strictly prohibited and will result in immediate permanent account termination and referral to appropriate law enforcement authorities. This applies to all content types including audio, video, imagery, and text posts.

ℹ Coming Soon — Parental Controls

The Parental Controls feature will let parents restrict specific artists' songs or albums for their child's account. This feature is in development and will be announced via a Platform update when available.
